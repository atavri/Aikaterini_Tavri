About Me

I specialize in microwave remote sensing of Arctic sea ice, focusing on synthetic aperture radar (SAR) analysis during the melt season. I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Brown University.

MOSAiC Expedition

In 2020, I participated in the historic MOSAiC Expedition, conducting in-situ optical and microwave remote sensing measurements aboard the research icebreaker Polarstern during the Arctic melt season.

Selected Publications
