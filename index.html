About Me

I specialize in microwave remote sensing of Arctic sea ice, focusing on synthetic aperture radar (SAR) analysis during the melt season. Currently Postdoctoral Research Associate at Brown University.

Research Highlights

  • Microwave remote sensing of Arctic sea ice
  • Polarimetric SAR scattering analysis during melt
  • Field campaigns for snow and sea ice property measurements

Selected Publications
