Aikaterini Tavri

Postdoctoral Research Associate, Brown University

About Me

I specialize in microwave remote sensing of Arctic sea ice, focusing on synthetic aperture radar (SAR) analysis during the melt season. Currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Brown University.

MOSAiC Expedition

In 2020, I participated in the historic MOSAiC Expedition, conducting ground-based optical and microwave measurements aboard the icebreaker Polarstern during the Arctic melt season.

Research Highlights

My work involves combining polarimetric SAR observations with field campaigns to improve understanding of sea ice geophysical processes under advanced melt conditions.
